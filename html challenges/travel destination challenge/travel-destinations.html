Here is the Top 3 Travel Destinations I want to visit!

  1. Northern Sweden

    The reason why I would LOVE to visit this place in particular is because of the absolute beauty you can get here at any time of the year. The entirety of northern Sweden (and the rest of Scandinavia) has been at the top of my list especially because of the hikes and nature reserves here.

  2. Australia

    I fell in love with Australia ever since I was a kid, because of the beaches and the more dry parts. Places like the Great Barrier Reef are just so so cool to me, and places nearer to the center like Uluru are of my interest too, due to the stargazing potential in those areas. I would love to visit, even if it gets super hot!

  3. Canada

    Canada, especially in the northern, more picturesque areas, has been on my list for a long time. I see Canada similar to the way I see northern Sweden, and for similar reasons. For example, the trails are stunning, and the wildlife is beautiful. I would love to visit Canada sometime!
